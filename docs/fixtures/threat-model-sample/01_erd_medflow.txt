--- Narrative (trust & sensitivity) ---

- PHI rests in PATIENT, CLINICAL_NOTE, NOTE_ATTACHMENT metadata, PROVIDER.npi, and AUDIT_LOG when it references patient context.
- Payment card data never stored raw; PAYMENT_METHOD keeps Stripe tokens only (PCI scope reduction).
- Video sessions use third-party vendor; video_room_id is opaque in APPOINTMENT — real-time media leaves this trust zone.
- External systems: Stripe (billing), OIDC IdP (workforce identity), object storage (attachments), LLM vendor (optional summarization — not persisted in this schema).
